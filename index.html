Hi, I'm Juliette! I'm a third year at Ohio State University with a double major in Computer Science and Linguistics.

My first encounter with programming was in 6th grade when my favorite artist created a story using Macromedia Flash. Enthralled by the interactivity, I went down the rabbit hole of creating silly games.

My decision to double major came about by chance. I had entered OSU with the intention of obtaining a major in Computer Science; when I signed up for a Linguistics class on a whim, little would I know that it would become my second love.

I am highly interested in the cognitive sciences, natural language processing, and software development and design.
